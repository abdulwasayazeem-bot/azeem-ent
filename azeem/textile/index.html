About Azeem Home Textile

Your Trusted Home & Institutional Textile Partner

Azeem Home Textile is the dedicated home textile division of Azeem Enterprises — a trading company with over 30 years of experience supplying industrial and consumer materials across Pakistan.

We supply a comprehensive range of home textile products including bed linen, comforters, mattress protectors, curtains, cushions, and institutional bedding to retailers, hotels, hospitals, and wholesalers across Pakistan. Our products combine quality materials, consistent finishing, and reliable supply — all at competitive pricing.

Whether you are sourcing for a single hotel property, a retail chain, or a hospital institution, Azeem Home Textile delivers the range, reliability, and pricing you need.

  • Full range of home and institutional textile products from one supplier
  • Consistent quality with rigorous sourcing and inspection standards
  • Bulk supply capability for hotels, hospitals, and retail chains
  • Custom sizing, branding, and packaging available on request
