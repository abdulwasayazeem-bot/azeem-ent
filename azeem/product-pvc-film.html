Transparent Soft PVC Film

Transparent Soft PVC Film is a versatile, crystal-clear flexible film made from plasticized polyvinyl chloride. Its exceptional clarity, flexibility, and durability make it one of the most widely used packaging and industrial films across multiple industries in Pakistan.

At Azeem Enterprises, we supply soft PVC film in a wide range of thicknesses and widths to meet the precise specifications of packaging converters, garment manufacturers, stationery producers, and medical device packagers.

The film offers excellent resistance to moisture, chemicals, and tearing, making it suitable for both protective and display applications. It can be supplied in rolls, sheets, or custom-cut formats with optional printing and lamination treatments.
