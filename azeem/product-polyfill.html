Polyfill (Wadding)

Polyfill — also known as wadding, polyester fill, or fiberfill — is a soft, fluffy polyester material used as filling or padding in a wide range of textile and furnishing applications. It is made from fine polyester fibres that are bonded together to create a lightweight, resilient, and lofty structure.

Azeem Enterprises supplies polyfill in multiple GSM grades (50–400 GSM), covering applications from lightweight quilt filling to dense upholstery padding and industrial insulation. Our polyfill is available in white, grey, and green, in roll widths up to 96 inches.

Polyfill is hypoallergenic, non-toxic, machine washable, and retains its loft even after repeated use — making it the preferred filling material for the home textile, furniture, toy manufacturing, and automotive industries across Pakistan.
