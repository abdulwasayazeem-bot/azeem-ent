Zippers

Zippers are one of the most essential fastening accessories in the garment, bag, luggage, and upholstery industries. At Azeem Enterprises, we supply a comprehensive range of zipper types — nylon coil zippers, metal zippers, and plastic moulded zippers — to garment manufacturers, bag makers, footwear brands, and home textile producers across Pakistan.

Our zipper range covers all standard sizes from fine #3 to heavy-duty #10, available in open-end, close-end, and two-way configurations. We supply in chain form (by the meter) or as finished zippers with pulls, stops, and sliders ready for direct use in production.

Available in a vast colour range to match any fabric or design requirement, our zippers are sourced from quality-certified manufacturers to ensure smooth operation, durability, and consistent tape quality — critical for high-volume garment and bag production.
