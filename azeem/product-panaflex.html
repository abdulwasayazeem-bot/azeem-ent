PVC Flex Vinyl (Panaflex)

PVC Flex Vinyl — commonly known by the trade name Panaflex — is a heavy-duty PVC coated fabric used as the primary substrate for outdoor advertising, billboards, banners, and large-format digital printing. It combines the structural strength of a woven polyester scrim with the printability and weather resistance of a PVC coating.

Azeem Enterprises supplies both frontlit and backlit PVC flex vinyl to the advertising, printing, and signage industries across Pakistan. Frontlit flex is used for externally illuminated billboards and banners, while backlit flex is optimised for lightboxes and internally illuminated signage where light transmission and even diffusion are critical.

Our PVC flex vinyl is available in standard widths from 2 feet to 16 feet and can be supplied in rolls of various lengths. It is compatible with all major solvent, eco-solvent, UV, and latex wide-format digital printers, providing vibrant colour output and long-lasting outdoor durability.
