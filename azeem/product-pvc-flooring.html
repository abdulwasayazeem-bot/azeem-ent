PVC Vinyl Flooring & Floor Material

PVC Vinyl Flooring is a multi-layer synthetic flooring product made primarily from polyvinyl chloride. It offers an exceptional combination of durability, water resistance, design versatility, and ease of installation — making it one of the fastest-growing flooring solutions for commercial, residential, and industrial applications in Pakistan.

Azeem Enterprises supplies PVC vinyl flooring in multiple formats: luxury vinyl tiles (LVT), vinyl planks, and vinyl rolls. Our range covers a wide spectrum of realistic wood, stone, and tile designs that achieve the aesthetic of natural materials at a significantly lower cost and with far greater practical performance.

PVC vinyl flooring is 100% waterproof, resistant to scratches and heavy foot traffic, easy to clean, and can be installed over most existing floor surfaces — making it ideal for offices, retail spaces, hospitals, schools, hotels, homes, and industrial workshops.
