Our Story

Three Decades of Trusted Supply

Azeem Enterprises was founded in Karachi in the early 1990s with a clear purpose: to become the most reliable single-source supplier of industrial materials for Pakistan's growing manufacturing sector. What began as a focused trading operation has grown, over three decades, into a comprehensive materials supply business trusted by manufacturers across the country.

Today, we supply over 15 product categories spanning polymer films, nonwoven fabrics, synthetic leather, protective packaging, flooring materials, home textiles, and garment accessories. Our customers range from packaging converters and garment manufacturers to automotive upholsterers and interior décor businesses — all united by a common need for consistent quality and dependable supply.

Headquartered in Karachi — Pakistan's commercial hub — we are strategically positioned to serve businesses nationwide. Our deep supplier relationships and decades of market knowledge allow us to deliver competitive pricing without compromising on the quality our customers rely on.

Our Journey

Three Decades of Growth

1990
Company Founded
Azeem Enterprises established in Karachi, initially focused on supplying packaging films to local manufacturers.
1998
Expanded into Nonwoven Fabrics
Added PP spunbond and laminated nonwoven fabrics to the portfolio, serving Pakistan's growing hygiene and packaging sectors.
2005
Synthetic Leather Range Launched
Entered the automotive and fashion markets with PVC and PU synthetic leather, serving upholsterers and garment manufacturers.
2012
Home Textile & Flooring Added
Diversified into home textile, polyfill, PVC vinyl flooring, and decorative PVC films, expanding our customer base significantly.
2020
15+ Product Categories
