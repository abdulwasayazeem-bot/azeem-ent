EVA Film

EVA Film — Ethylene Vinyl Acetate film — is a highly flexible, transparent thermoplastic material renowned for its excellent clarity, low-temperature flexibility, and strong adhesive bonding properties when heat-activated. It occupies a unique position between rigid plastics and rubber, offering the best characteristics of both.

Azeem Enterprises supplies EVA film for a diverse range of applications. In the footwear industry, EVA is the primary material for shoe soles and insoles due to its cushioning and lightweight properties. In lamination applications, EVA film serves as the bonding interlayer between glass panels, solar cells, and safety glass assemblies.

Our EVA film is available in a range of thicknesses, VA (vinyl acetate) content levels, and widths — allowing precise matching to the performance requirements of your specific application, whether that is flexibility, adhesion strength, optical clarity, or UV resistance.
