Synthetic Leather

PVC / PU Synthetic Leather

Synthetic Leather — available in both PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) and PU (Polyurethane) formulations — is a man-made alternative to genuine leather that combines durability, aesthetic versatility, and cost-effectiveness. It is used extensively across automotive, fashion, footwear, and furniture industries worldwide.

PVC Synthetic Leather offers excellent durability, water resistance, and ease of cleaning, making it the preferred choice for automotive seat covers, upholstery, bags, and shoes. PU Synthetic Leather provides a softer, more natural feel with better breathability — ideal for premium fashion accessories, garments, and upholstery.

At Azeem Enterprises, we supply both grades in a wide range of textures, embossed patterns, colours, and finishes. Whether you need a smooth high-gloss finish for a luxury bag or a grained automotive texture for seat upholstery, we can source the right material for your application.
