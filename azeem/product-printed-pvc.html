Printed & Embossed PVC Films

Printed and Embossed PVC Films are decorative surface materials produced by applying patterns, textures, or printed designs onto PVC film substrates. These films transform plain surfaces into visually rich, tactile finishes that mimic wood grain, leather, marble, fabric weaves, and abstract patterns.

Azeem Enterprises supplies printed and embossed PVC films for the furniture industry (cabinet wrapping, MDF board lamination), interior décor (wall panels, ceilings), automotive trim, and stationery products. These films are typically laminated onto MDF, plywood, or foam boards using standard lamination equipment.

Available in an extensive range of designs and surface textures — from high-gloss wood grains to matte concrete effects — our decorative PVC films allow manufacturers and designers to achieve premium aesthetics at a fraction of the cost of real wood or stone finishes.
